# Promoting Your Open Source Project

Publishing code is only the first step. For your project to reach the agencies, engineers, and communities who could benefit from it, people need to know it exists, understand what it does, and feel invited to participate. Promotion isn't about marketing — it's about connecting human-centered work with the people it can serve.

## Why Promotion Matters

- **Adoption**: Projects that solve real problems for real people deserve to reach others facing similar challenges
- **Contributions**: A visible project attracts contributors who can make it better for everyone
- **Nava's mission**: Sharing our work broadly multiplies the public benefit — one improvement can ripple across agencies
- **Community building**: Active promotion strengthens the civic tech ecosystem we all depend on
