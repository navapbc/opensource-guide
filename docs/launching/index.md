# Launch Considerations

The services we build serve different purposes — from direct-to-public benefit applications to the shared infrastructure that makes those applications possible. Different types of projects have different needs when going public, and getting the details right protects both the people who use these services and the agencies we partner with.

While the core [publishing process](../publishing/index.md) applies to all projects, these guides cover additional considerations based on your project's nature.

## Project Types at Nava

### [Government Technology Projects](government-projects.md)
Projects built in partnership with government agencies. These require careful attention to data sensitivity, compliance, and the collaborative relationship with our agency partners.

### [Infrastructure and Platform Projects](infrastructure.md)
Shared infrastructure, platform tools, and DevOps-oriented projects that build capacity across teams — requiring attention to security scanning, configuration management, and operational documentation.
